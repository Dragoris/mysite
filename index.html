About Me

Hey, my name is Matt and I've become fascinated by the internet and it's ability to facilitate shared ideas across the world. For years I enjoyed everything the web has to offer without ever questioning why or how it all worked. It wasn't until I started drilling down into those questions that I got hooked on trying to get my own creations on the web for the world to see. After studying up on how all the pieces fit together I realized web development wasn't done through some unattainable sorcery (even though it still sometimes feels like it!), it's something I can actually do.

My evenings and weekends quickly became consumed by learning the fundamentals of web and how I can interact with it. These studies in turn inspired me to tackle my first website, LazyDJ, and implement the concepts I've read about. My site you are visiting today is where I'll be sharing with you what I've made and what I've learned, I hope you enjoy.

When I'm not digging into the internet you'll find me playing basketball with my team, marveling at the Warriors' unselfish style of play and chemistry, or backpacking through the northern California wilderness with friends and family.
